DEPARTMENT SPECIAL PROGRAM

The curriculum prescribed by the Department of Education (DepEd) is generally designed for the average learners. While the school adopts this program, it also recognizes individual differences among the students.To respond to the needs, interests and abilities of the learners, special programs are provided.

WHAT WE EXPECT FROM OUR GRADUATES

USJ-R Elementary graduates are expected to be academically competitive, good communicators, and socially engaging.

SYSTEMATIC

Able to apply knowledge of computing fundamentals and specialization contributing to the needs of the community

ANALYTIC
